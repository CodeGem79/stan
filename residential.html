Residential Cleaning

At Starglow Cleaners Ltd, we provide professional residential cleaning services designed to support busy households that value a clean, comfortable, and well cared for home. We understand that inviting a cleaning service into your home requires trust. That is why we focus on consistency, attention to detail, and a respectful approach in every property we clean.

Personalized Cleaning Plans for Your Family

No two homes are the same. We offer bespoke cleaning checklists tailored to your specific priorities. Do you have pets? We focus on hair removal and non-toxic sanitization. Have young children? We ensure all surfaces are cleaned with eco-friendly products that are safe for little ones.

Our Residential Cleaning Services

Our residential cleaning service can be tailored to suit your lifestyle and home, whether you require regular support or a one off clean. Services typically include:

  • Cleaning of kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, and living areas.
  • Dusting and wiping of surfaces, skirting boards, and high touch areas.
  • Vacuuming and mopping of floors.
  • Bin removal and light tidying.
  • Careful cleaning using professional products

Professional Standards You Can Trust

Our cleaners are fully trained in professional chemical handling and follow structured cleaning processes to ensure consistent results. We take care to treat every home with respect, using appropriate products and methods for different surfaces and finishes.
