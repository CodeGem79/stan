AirBnB & Short-Stay Cleaning

Ensuring 5-Star Guest Reviews for Your Property in South East London

Hotel-Standard Cleaning for the Modern Host

Managing a short-term rental is a full-time job. At Star Glow, we take the stress out of guest turnovers. Our specialized AirBnB cleaning service is designed to ensure your property is spotless, sanitized, and perfectly presented for every new arrival. We understand that cleanliness is the #1 factor in guest reviews.

We don't just "clean"—we prepare an experience. Our team follows a rigorous checklist tailored to short-stay rentals, including professional linen changes, staging of towels and toiletries, and a deep clean of high-traffic areas like kitchens and bathrooms.

Reliable Turnarounds Between Guest Stays

At Starglow Cleaners Ltd, we specialise in professional cleaning services for Airbnb and short stay properties. We understand that short stay cleaning is not just about cleanliness. It is about presentation, consistency, and protecting guest experience. Our service is designed to support hosts who need reliable, high quality cleans between guest stays, often within tight turnaround times. From single listings to multi property portfolios, we deliver a service that allows hosts to operate with confidence and ease.

What Our Airbnb and Short Stay Cleaning Includes:

  • Full cleaning of bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchens, and living areas.
  • Bed making using host provided linen.
  • Careful cleaning of high touch areas.
  • Bin removal and light property reset.
  • Same day turnaround where required.
  • Photo confirmation after each clean.

We operate on a subscription based model, offering predictable monthly pricing and guaranteed availability. This approach removes the uncertainty of last minute bookings and ensures your property is always supported by a reliable cleaning partner.

Our subscription services are ideal for:

  • Regularly booked Airbnb listings.
  • Professional short stay hosts
  • Co hosts and property managers
  • Hosts seeking consistency and peace of mind.

Built for Consistency and Quality:
