Residential Cleaning

Professional home cleaning services across South East London.

Reclaim Your Time with a Spotless, Glowing Home

Your home should be your sanctuary, not a source of stress. Star Glow provides premium residential cleaning services designed to fit seamlessly into your lifestyle. Whether you need a weekly refresh or a deep monthly clean, our team handles the hard work so you can focus on the things that truly matter.

Our residential service is built on trust and consistency. We understand that inviting someone into your personal space is a big decision, which is why all our cleaners are fully vetted, background-checked, and trained to respect your privacy and belongings. We treat every home as if it were our own, ensuring a meticulous finish every time.

Personalized Cleaning Plans for Your Family

No two homes are the same. We offer bespoke cleaning checklists tailored to your specific priorities. Do you have pets? We focus on hair removal and non-toxic sanitization. Have young children? We ensure all surfaces are cleaned with eco-friendly products that are safe for little ones.

Our Standard Residential Clean Includes:

  • Living Areas: Dusting all surfaces, vacuuming, and floor mopping.
  • Kitchen: Sanitizing countertops, cleaning appliance exteriors, and sink polishing.
  • Bathrooms: Deep scrubbing of toilets, showers, tubs, and mirrors.
  • Bedrooms: Dusting furniture, making beds, and floor care.
  • General: Emptying bins and wiping down high-touch switches and handles.

Eco-Friendly Cleaning for a Healthier Home

We prioritize the health of your family and the planet. Star Glow exclusively uses high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning solutions that effectively eliminate bacteria and allergens without leaving behind harsh chemical fumes or residues. Experience a fresh, natural clean that smells as good as it looks.
