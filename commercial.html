Commercial Cleaning

Professional Workspace Solutions in South East London

Create a Healthier, More Productive Environment for Your Team

In the modern business world, a clean office is more than just aesthetics; it is a vital component of employee health and corporate professionalism. Star Glow provides bespoke commercial cleaning contracts tailored to the unique needs of your facility, whether you run a high-traffic retail space, a quiet corporate office, or a local community hub.

Our team is trained to handle the specific demands of commercial environments, including high-touch point sanitization, specialized floor care, and discreet service. We work around your business hours—offering early morning, late evening, or weekend slots—to ensure your operations are never disrupted.

Reliable Contracts & Dedicated Account Management

Consistency is the hallmark of Star Glow. When you sign a commercial contract with us, you receive a dedicated account manager and a vetted cleaning team that gets to know your premises inside and out. We use professional-grade, eco-friendly cleaning agents that provide a deep clean without leaving behind harsh chemical odors.

Our Commercial Checklist includes:

  • Sanitization of all workstations, keyboards, and telephones.
  • Deep cleaning of communal kitchens and breakrooms.
  • Professional restroom hygiene management and restocking.
  • Floor maintenance (vacuuming, mopping, and buffing).
  • Waste management and eco-friendly recycling disposal.
